The Narrative

I am a researcher and educator bridging 12 years of pedagogical practice with a scholarly focus on the "underground" origins of our digital world. My research investigates how expertise survives the transition from informal, radical spaces to formal institutional structures.

Research & Digital Works

From BBS to Blackboard: The Pedagogy of the Exploit

STS Corpus Linguistics Voyant Tools

This research evaluates technical knowledge transfer through a longitudinal analysis of Phrack Magazine. Utilizing Voyant Tools, the study maps how technical identity is negotiated within decentralized networks. The analysis defines hacker rhetoric as a structured, high-stakes instructional system.
